Every major booking platform will tell you they're the best option for barbers. They're all telling the truth — and they're all leaving out the same thing.

Here's a straight comparison of the main options, and the feature category none of them offer.

## The Main Platforms

### Booksy

The most barber-specific platform on this list. Strong on discovery — clients can find you through the Booksy marketplace, which helps with new client acquisition. Calendar management is solid. Reminders go out automatically.

**Best for:** Barbers who want to grow their new client base through a marketplace.

**Gap:** Zero outbound reactivation. If a client stops booking, Booksy does nothing. They wait for the client to come back. They never will for a meaningful percentage of your lapsed list.

### Square Appointments

Cleanest UX. Integrates seamlessly with Square payments, which most barbers are already using for card processing. Easy to set up, reliable.

**Best for:** Barbers who want the simplest possible scheduling + payment setup.

**Gap:** Same issue. Square is a waiting room. It's great at managing the clients who already come to you. It does nothing for the ones who stopped.

### GlossGenius

Popular in the booth-rent space. Stronger on the business dashboard side — revenue tracking, analytics. Looks sharp. Has a client-facing app.

**Best for:** Barbers who want detailed reporting and a polished client-facing experience.

**Gap:** Again — no outbound recovery. The analytics will show you exactly how many clients lapsed and exactly how much revenue you're missing. Then it stops.
